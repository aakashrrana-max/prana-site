Meet the doctor

Hi, I'm Dr. Aakash Rana.

I trained at New York University, one of the most respected dental schools in the world, and I've since put over $100,000 into continuing education so my patients always get the best, most current care. I built Prana to be the kind of practice I'd want for my own family — where one dentist actually knows you, listens first, explains everything in plain language, and never judges.

When I'm not with patients, I spend a lot of time mentoring younger dentists and teaching them new techniques. Outside the office, I'm big on fitness, love to travel, and try to enjoy every part of life — and I bring that same energy to the chair every day.
