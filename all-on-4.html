What is All-on-4?

All-on-4 secures a full arch of fixed teeth on just four implants — a stable, immovable foundation built from a simple three-part system.

1

The implant

A titanium post placed in the jaw acts like a natural tooth root — the anchor that holds everything in place.

2

The abutment

A connector on each implant that passes through the gum and links the implant to your new arch of teeth.

3

The arch

Your visible new teeth — acrylic or zirconia, shaped and shaded to you, fixed firmly onto the abutments.

Common questions

How much is All-on-4?

From $9,999 per arch, with a written plan — no surprises. Financing brings it to roughly $167/mo on approved credit. Your exact price is confirmed in writing at your free consult.

Is it really done in one day?

Yes. After your workup, Dr. Rana places your implants and you leave the same day with a fixed provisional set. Your final acrylic or zirconia arch is fitted once you've healed, usually in 3–5 months.

Will I leave without teeth?

No. You always leave the same day with a fixed provisional set of teeth.

Acrylic or zirconia?

Acrylic is lighter on cost and a great-value fixed arch; zirconia is the most durable and natural-looking. We'll help you choose at your consult.

Am I a candidate if I've been told no before?

Often, yes. Most people who come in for a consult are candidates — simple and complex cases alike. Even with significant bone loss, a quick 3D scan is the only way to know for sure.

Does it hurt?

Most patients are surprised how comfortable it is. Sedation is available and we go entirely at your pace, with a clear recovery plan afterward.

How is Prana different from the big implant centers?

You see Dr. Rana at every visit, in a calm boutique setting — not a rotating roster at a high-volume corporate center. Same premium materials and same-day teeth, with an in-house lab, transparent pricing, and a price thousands less.
