Emergencies, answered.

Can I be seen today?

Yes. We hold same-day emergency slots daily and welcome walk-ins, Mon-Sat 8AM-8PM. Call (786) 579-0521 and we'll get you in.

How much does an emergency visit cost?

Your emergency exam and X-ray are free, so you understand the problem and the cost before any treatment is decided.

What counts as a dental emergency?

Toothaches, broken or chipped teeth, knocked-out teeth, swelling or infection, lost fillings or crowns, and bleeding. When in doubt, call us.

What should I do for a knocked-out tooth?

Keep it moist (in milk or saliva), avoid touching the root, and call us immediately, time matters.
