Appointments & visits

Getting in to see us.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

Appointments are recommended so we can reserve time for you, but walk-ins are always welcome for dental emergencies during our hours, Monday–Saturday, 8AM–8PM.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. Your first consultation, including a CT scan and digital smile preview, is completely free, with no obligation. That's over $700 in services at no cost.

Is Dr. Rana the one who treats me?

Yes. Dr. Aakash Rana personally plans and performs every procedure at Prana Dental, from your first consultation to your final result. He is NYU-trained, with over $100,000 invested in continuing education.

Do you see children and families?

Yes — Prana is a family practice. We care for kids, teens and adults, all in a calm, judgment-free studio with a fully bilingual team.

How often should I come in for a checkup?

Most patients do best with a checkup and cleaning every six months, but Dr. Rana will recommend the right schedule for your mouth — no upselling, just honest guidance.

Pricing & insurance

Clear costs, no surprises.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We accept most major dental insurances and will help you maximize your benefits. We also offer 0% financing for treatments not covered by insurance.

How much is a checkup and cleaning?

Your first visit includes a free consultation worth $700, with a 3D scan. We give you clear, written pricing before any treatment, accept most major dental insurance, and offer 0% financing for anything not covered. See full details on our pricing page.

Why is pricing lower here than other Miami clinics?

We run our own in-house lab, so there's no outsourcing markup. That means faster turnaround, better fit, and lower cost passed directly to you, without compromising quality.

Financing

Care that fits your budget.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, 0% interest plans are available for qualified patients, with payments starting at $167/month and no down payment required on approved credit. Applying does not affect your credit score.

What financing options do you work with?

We partner with trusted lenders including CareCredit, Cherry and LendingClub to offer 0% interest plans on approved credit. See your options on our financing page.

Treatments & comfort

Gentle care, start to finish.

Will it hurt?

Comfort is a priority. We offer oral conscious sedation and gentle, modern techniques so most patients are surprised by how comfortable and quick their treatment is.

I get anxious at the dentist. Can you help?

Absolutely. We move at your pace, explain everything in plain language, and oral conscious sedation is available so most patients are surprised by how easy it is.

Do you place dental implants and All-on-4?

Yes. Dr. Rana plans and places single dental implants as well as full-arch All-on-4 restorations, all digitally guided and crafted with our in-house lab.

Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome for emergencies during our hours, Monday–Saturday, 8AM–8PM. Call us at (786) 579-0521 and we'll get you seen as quickly as possible — see our emergency dental page.
