Questions

Financing, answered.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We offer flexible monthly payment plans through trusted partners like CareCredit, Cherry and LendingClub, with payments starting from $167/month on approved credit. Most decisions come back in minutes.

Is it really 0%?

Yes — 0% interest plans are available for qualified patients on approved credit. We'll walk you through the exact terms at your free consult so there are no surprises. Individual plans vary and are subject to credit approval.

Will applying hurt my credit?

No. Checking your options uses a soft credit check that does not affect your credit score, so you can see what you qualify for before committing to anything.

Is there a down payment?

For most qualified patients there's no down payment required on approved credit. We'll confirm your exact terms in writing before any treatment begins.

Do you take insurance?

Yes. We accept most major dental insurance and help you get the most from your benefits. Financing can cover anything your plan doesn't, so cost never stands between you and care.

What if I'm not approved?

You have options. We work with several financing partners, so if one doesn't fit we can explore another, plus in-house arrangements and insurance. Our team will help you find a path that works.
