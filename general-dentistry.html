Questions

Family dentistry, answered.

How much is a checkup and cleaning?

Your first visit includes a free consultation worth $700 with a 3D scan. We give you clear, written pricing before any treatment, accept most major dental insurance, and offer 0% financing for anything not covered.

Do you see children and families?

Yes — Prana is a family practice. We care for kids, teens and adults in a calm, judgment-free studio with a fully bilingual team.

How often should I come in?

Most patients do best with a checkup and cleaning every six months, but Dr. Rana recommends the right schedule for your mouth, with no upselling.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major dental insurances and help you get the most from your benefits. For anything not covered, 0% financing is available.

I get anxious at the dentist. Can you help?

Absolutely. We move at your pace, explain everything, and oral conscious sedation is available so most patients are surprised how easy it is.
