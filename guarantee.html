What the guarantee covers

Workmanship on implants

For 5 years from placement, if a covered implant or its restoration fails due to a defect in our workmanship, we'll repair or replace it at no charge for the workmanship.

Our in-house lab work

Because crowns, bridges and full-arch restorations are designed and milled in our own lab, we control the quality and stand behind the fit and finish.

How it works

What exactly is covered?

The guarantee covers the workmanship of implants and restorations placed at Prana Dental — the placement and the fit/finish of the restoration we crafted. Specific coverage for your case is written into your treatment plan.

What's not covered?

Normal wear, accidental damage or trauma, changes caused by an unrelated medical condition, and failure to keep recommended checkups or follow home-care and aftercare instructions are not covered. Third-party or previously placed dental work done elsewhere is not covered.

What do I need to do to keep it valid?

Keep your recommended hygiene visits and follow the aftercare plan we give you. Staying on top of routine checkups lets us catch and address anything early — and keeps your guarantee in force.

How do I make a claim?

Just call us at (786) 579-0521 or message us on WhatsApp. We'll bring you in, assess the issue, and make it right per the terms in your treatment plan.
