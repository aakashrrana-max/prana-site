How much is a dental implant in Miami?

Implants start at $1,850 for the implant and custom crown. Final price depends on bone, gum health, and whether an extraction or sedation is needed, confirmed in writing at your free consult.

Does it hurt?

Most patients are surprised how comfortable it is. Oral sedation is available, and we go at your pace.

Can it be done in one visit?

Often, yes, depending on the site. We confirm at your free 3D scan.

How long do implants last?

With good care, implants can last decades. We back our workmanship with a 5-year guarantee.

What if I'm missing several teeth?

We may recommend an implant bridge or All-on-4 full-arch. We'll walk you through the best option at your consult.
