Dr. Aakash Rana, DDS

Meet the doctor.

Hi, I’m Dr. Aakash Rana, a Miami dentist who got into this for one reason: I love helping people feel good about their smile again. If the dentist makes you anxious, you’re not alone, and you’re in good hands. I take the time to listen, explain things in plain language, and never rush or judge.

Some of the work I’m proudest of has been on dental mission trips, treating families in Guatemala, the Dominican Republic, and at Florida’s Mission of Mercy who had waited years for relief. That same care is exactly what I want every patient to feel here in Miami.

I trained at New York University, one of the most respected dental schools in the world, and I’ve since put over $100,000 into continuing education so my patients always get the best, most current care.

When I’m not with patients, I spend a lot of time mentoring younger dentists and teaching them new techniques. Outside the office, I’m big on fitness, love to travel, and try to enjoy every part of life.

Guatemala, the Dominican Republic, Mission of Mercy, and right here at home, the same standard of care for everyone.
