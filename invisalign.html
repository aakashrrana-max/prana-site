Questions

Straightening, answered.

How much do clear aligners or braces cost?

Orthodontic treatment at Prana starts from $99/month on approved credit. Because every case is different, your exact plan is confirmed in writing at your free consult, and 0% financing options are available for anything not covered by insurance.

Are clear aligners or braces better for me?

Both work beautifully. Clear aligners are removable and nearly invisible, ideal for adults and teens who want discretion, while braces can be the better fit for more complex movements. Dr. Rana recommends what suits your case after your 3D scan.

How long does treatment take?

Most cases finish in 6 to 18 months depending on how much movement is needed. You'll see your projected timeline in a 3D preview before you begin.

Do you treat teens?

Yes — we treat teens and adults with both clear aligners and braces, in a calm, judgment-free studio with a fully bilingual team.

Does it hurt?

You may feel mild pressure for a day or two when you switch aligners or adjust braces — that's your teeth moving. It settles quickly, and we keep you comfortable throughout.

Do you accept insurance and offer financing?

We accept most major dental insurance and offer 0% financing on approved credit from $99/month through CareCredit, Cherry and LendingClub, with no impact to your credit to apply.
