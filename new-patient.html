Questions

The free first visit, answered.

Is the first visit really free?

Yes. New patients get a genuinely free first visit — a consultation with Dr. Rana, a 3D CT scan and a digital smile preview, a $700+ value. There's no obligation to book any treatment.

What's the catch?

There isn't one. We'd rather earn your trust than charge you to walk in the door. You'll leave with a clear, honest plan and a written quote, and the decision is entirely yours, on your timeline.

What should I bring?

Just yourself, your insurance card if you have dental coverage, and a list of any questions or concerns. We handle the rest, including your 3D scan and exam.

Do you take insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans and help you get the most from your benefits. For anything not covered, 0% financing is available on approved credit, with payments from $167/mo.

Can I bring my kids or family?

Absolutely. Prana is a family practice, and the free first visit applies to every new patient — kids, teens and adults alike, all with a fully bilingual team.

How long does it take?

Plan for about 45 to 60 minutes. That includes your consultation with Dr. Rana, your 3D scan and exam, and time to talk through your options with no rush.
