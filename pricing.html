Questions

Pricing, answered.

Why are your prices lower than other Miami dentists?

We design and craft crowns, veneers and restorations in our own in-house lab, so we skip the outside-lab markup and pass the savings to you — without ever cutting quality. One NYU-trained dentist, fairer prices.

Is the first-visit consultation really free?

Yes. Your first-visit consult is free — a $700 value including a 3D scan, exam and an honest, written treatment plan, with zero obligation.

Do you take dental insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans and help you get the most from your benefits. For anything not covered, 0% financing is available.

Can I finance my treatment?

Absolutely. We offer 0% interest plans on approved credit, with payments starting at $167/month, through CareCredit, Cherry and LendingClub. Checking your rate doesn't affect your credit.
