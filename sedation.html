Is this for you?

Sedation might be right for you.

There's nothing to be embarrassed about. If any of these sound like you, sedation can turn a dreaded visit into one you barely remember.

◇

Dental anxiety

Past bad experiences or a deep fear of the chair — we go slowly, explain everything, and help you relax.

◇

Sensitive gag reflex

If scans, impressions or treatment trigger your gag reflex, sedation helps your body stay calm and at ease.

◇

Difficulty getting numb

Some people are hard to numb. Sedation keeps you comfortable while we make sure you feel nothing.

◇

Longer or multiple procedures

Combine treatments into one relaxed visit instead of many — time passes easily and you save trips.

◇

Low pain tolerance

If you're especially sensitive, sedation takes the edge off so the whole experience feels gentle.

◇

You just want to relax

You don't need a reason. If you'd simply rather drift through your appointment in comfort, we've got you.

Questions

Sedation, answered.

Is sedation dentistry safe?

Yes. Oral conscious sedation has a long track record of safety. We review your health history first, tailor the dose to you, and monitor you closely throughout your visit, so you can relax knowing you're in careful hands.

Will I be asleep?

No. With oral conscious sedation you stay awake and able to respond — you're conscious the entire time, just deeply relaxed and at ease. It's not general anesthesia; you're simply calm and comfortable.

Will I remember the appointment?

Many patients remember very little of the treatment itself. Most describe the visit as feeling quick and easy, like the time simply passed by.

Can I drive after sedation?

No. The effects linger for several hours, so you'll need a trusted friend or family member to drive you home. We'll tell you exactly what to expect when you book.

Who's a good candidate?

Sedation suits patients with dental anxiety, a sensitive gag reflex, difficulty getting numb, low pain tolerance, or anyone facing a longer or multiple procedure. Dr. Rana reviews your health at your free consult to confirm it's right for you.

How much does it cost, and does insurance cover it?

Sedation is an affordable add-on to your treatment, and we confirm the exact cost in writing at your free consult. Many plans contribute, and 0% financing from $167/month is available for anything not covered.
