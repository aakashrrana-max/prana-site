Questions

Veneers, answered.

How much do veneers cost in Miami?

Porcelain veneers start at $999 per veneer at Prana. Because every smile is different, your exact plan is confirmed in writing at your free smile-design consult, and flexible monthly financing is available.

Will my veneers look fake?

No. We design them to your face and let you preview the result digitally before any work begins, so you approve the look first. Shape and shade are matched to you.

How long does it take?

Because our lab is in-house, most cases finish in just a couple of visits, far faster than practices that outsource to an outside lab.

Does it hurt?

Most patients are surprised how comfortable it is. We work gently, oral sedation is available, and we never rush you.

How do I keep them looking good?

You care for veneers just like natural teeth, brushing, flossing, and regular cleanings. We'll show you exactly how.

What if I only need one or two?

That's completely fine. Many patients only treat a few teeth. We'll recommend the minimum that gets you the result you want.
