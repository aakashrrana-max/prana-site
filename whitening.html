Questions

Whitening, answered.

How much does professional whitening cost?

Professional whitening at Prana starts around $299, confirmed at your free consult. We give you clear, written pricing before anything begins, and 0% financing is available for anything not covered.

How much whiter will my teeth get?

Most patients see several shades of even brightening. At your free consult we check your starting shade and set a realistic, natural-looking goal for your teeth.

Will it make my teeth sensitive?

Because treatment is supervised by Dr. Rana with dentist-grade gels and desensitizing steps, most patients have little to no sensitivity. We also offer a sensitivity-friendly plan.

How long do results last?

With custom take-home trays for occasional touch-ups and good habits, results can last a long time. We'll build a simple maintenance plan so your smile stays bright.

Is whitening safe for my enamel?

Yes. Dentist-supervised whitening with professional gels and custom-fit trays is designed to protect your enamel — far safer than one-size-fits-all drugstore strips.

Can I whiten with veneers or crowns?

Whitening only changes natural teeth, not veneers or crowns. We'll plan your shade carefully so your natural teeth and any restorations stay beautifully matched.
